Welcome to Township Business Directory

Township Business Directory is your gateway to discovering local businesses in South Africa's vibrant townships. Our mission is to empower township businesses by providing a platform where they can connect with their communities, showcase their products and services, and grow their customer base.

Based in Orange Farm, we have partnered with a wide range of businesses, from local car washes to retail shops, helping them thrive and contribute to the local economy. Our platform leverages the power of technology to make township business discovery easy and accessible.

Whether you're a local resident looking to support small businesses or a business owner wanting to list your services, the Township Business Directory is here to assist. Join us on this journey towards sustainable growth and economic empowerment for townships across South Africa.
